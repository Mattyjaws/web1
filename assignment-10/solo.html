Going alone to your first festival?
1
Stay hydrated!
Make sure to stay hydrated throughout the festival, nothing is worse than having to cut your night short because you pass out from dehydration. Fill up between dj sets if you absolutely have to, you never know how much water you will need for different sets.
2
Meet new people!
You are here by yourself and around many other people, make new friends and dance with people! Introduce yourself because everyone aroung you is there for the same reason, you already have something in common.
3
enjoy the music!
You paid a lot of money to be here, enjoy your time there! Don't worry about being there by yourself because everyone you are around is there for the same reason. Get lost in the music!
4
EAT!
Eat something either at the festival or before the festival. You will feel sick and be out of energy quickly if you don't replenish your energy with a large meal, you will be dancing almost 6 or more hours at the festival.
5
get rest!
Get enough sleep after each day, nothing sucks worse than not being able to enjoy a full day at a festival you paid for because you didn't get enough sleep. The moment you get back to your hotel or campsite go to sleep.
